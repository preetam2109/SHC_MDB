About

The Ayushman Arogya Mandir (AAM) DPDMIS Portal, developed by Chhattisgarh Medical Services Corporation Limited (CGMSCL), aims to transform the supply, distribution, tracking ,consumption and ensuring last-mile delivery of drugs and consumables. ... Read More This will help to analyse the availability of essential drugs and ensure accurate stock at the AAM/SHC level. Approx 4400 Ayushman Arogya Mandirs across the Chhattisgarh state providing universal and free Comprehensive Primary Health Care (CPHC). These centers focus on wellness and offer a basic range of health services, including maternal and child healthcare, non-communicable disease care, palliative and rehabilitative care, oral, eye, and ENT care, mental health services, and first-level care for primary health care services. The new DPDMIS portal for Ayushman Arogya Mandir in Chhattisgarh will support this ambitious healthcare initiative by National Health Mission Chhattisgarh(NHM) for ensuring efficient supply chain management, enabling the consistent availability of essential drugs and medical supplies till last mile.
