About

The Vendor Registration System (VRS) is an online platform developed to streamline and simplify the supplier registration and document management process for pharmaceutical vendors. ... Read More

This system enables suppliers to register once and upload all essential statutory and technical documents such as:

  • Manufacturing and Wholesale Licenses
  • WHO-GMP / COPP Certificates
  • Non-Conviction Certificates
  • Turnover Certificates
  • GST Registration and PAN
  • Product Authorization and other relevant documents
Once uploaded and validated, these documents remain available for all future tenders ‚Äî eliminating the need to re-upload the same files repeatedly during each bidding process.
